Shahrukh Khan

The King Khan of Bollywood

Here's a time line of Shahrukh Khan's life:

  • 1965 - Born in New Delhi, India
  • 1989 - Debut on television through fauji
  • 1992 - Debut in films through Deewana
  • 1993-1994 - Anti-hero roles in Dar and Baazigar
  • 1995-1998 - Did Romantic Roles
  • 1999-2003 - Career Challenges as his films didnt work well
  • 2004-2009 - Resurgence period. Won various awards for his films released in this phase
  • 2010-2015 - Expansion to action and comedy
  • 2015-present - Career fluctuations, films under-performing
